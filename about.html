About me

Ever since I was a child, my interests have always been split in two - maths and arts. It seemed unusual for many people around me, but only since I grew up I realised that one doesn't necessarily exclude the other one. One can be good at logic and also creativity. The passion for both of these aspects has led me to always explore the data visually and look for creative solutions.
