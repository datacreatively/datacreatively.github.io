data . . .

Who said that data and creativity cannot go together?
For many, the words 'data', 'analytics' or 'numbers' bring up the fearful memory of the mathematics class at highschool, where the tasks were difficult and the concepts impossible to grasp while wondering, "...when will I ever need this?".
And yet, data is all around us whether we admit it or not. Small portion of it is captured by machines for analysts and scientists to bring insights and solutions. A part is also captured by our brains and helps us to survive, interact, and make our daily decisions. The more creatively we use these data, the more variety we can bring to solve our problems and the higher chances to find a suitable solution!

Hopefully the posts here will inspire you to get creative with data and help you to overcome problems I came across when working on my projects. Enjoy!
