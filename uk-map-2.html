Purpose of the project

The idea to create the map of earthquakes came after my boyfriend needed one for his project. Despite I haven't created a map from scratch before, I decided to take the challenge and produce a map that would show all the important information and at the same time would be easy to understand.

The guide on how to create the basic map (including data collection and transformation) is described here.
